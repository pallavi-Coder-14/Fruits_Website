Fruits and Their Benefits

Fruits are an important part of a healthy diet. They provide vitamins, minerals, and natural energy to the human body.

Apple

Apples are sweet and crunchy fruits. They are good for digestion and help keep the heart healthy. Apple has for the most part been the world's largest company by market capitalization, and, as of 2024, is the largest manufacturing company by revenue, the fourth-largest personal computer vendor, the largest vendor of tablet computers, and the largest vendor of mobile phones.

Benefits of Apple

  • Rich in fiber
  • Good for heart
  • Improves digestion

Banana
